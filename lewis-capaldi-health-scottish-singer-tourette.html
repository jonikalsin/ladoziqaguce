Lewis Capaldi Biography

Lewis Capaldi was born on October 7, 1996, and raised in a middle-class household in Glasgow, Scotland, in the United Kingdom. His family departed the city four years after his birth. He is known to be of Scottish descent and to follow the Christian faith. He is descended from Scottish, Irish, and Italian ancestors.

He finished his primary education at St. Kentigern’s Academy in Blackburn, West Lothian, Scotland.

Following that, he enrolled at New College Lanarkshire in Motherwell, Scotland, where he graduated with an Honours Degree in Music.

During his school and college years, he was less of a scholar and more into extracurricular activities, and he was well-known as an entertainer who pulled many pranks.
