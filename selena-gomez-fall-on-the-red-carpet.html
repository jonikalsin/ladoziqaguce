Selena Gomez Falls On The Red Carpet! What happened?

Source: IG

  • Selena Gomez presented an award on the SAG Award 2022 barefoot
  • She wore a black gown designed by Oscar de la Renta at the 28th SAG Award
  • Sal accidentally fell on the SAG Awards red carpet by tripping her heel

Selena Gomez couldn’t keep her heels on!

Selena Gomez made her first red carpet appearance in 2022, stepping out at the Screen Actors Guild Awards. On Sunday evening (February 28), the 28th SAG Awards was held at the Barker Hangar in Santa Monica, California.

Unfortunately, the event night became a bit clumsy for her as she accidentally fell on the SAG Awards red carpet. Her heel caused her to trip and fall to her knees while walking on the carpet.

Thankfully, Gomez did not suffer any major injuries, someone was there to catch her and help her literally get back on her feet.

After that, she removed her other side heels and carried them away. Even she went barefoot on the stage accompanied by Only Murders in the Building co-star Martin Short to present the award for outstanding performance by a female actor in a supporting role. The event was broadcast live on both TNT and TBS at 8:00 p.m. EST/ 5:00 p.m PST.

The cast of “CODA” earned the biggest award of the night for best motion picture ensemble. Similarly, Squid Game made history by becoming the first non-English series and first Korean series to be nominated for Best Drama series ensemble.

SAG Awards 2022: Selena Gomez’s stunning looked

For the event, Selena Gomez sported in a black column gown designed by Oscar de la Renta. Along with her gorgeous gown, she wore $1 million Bulgari jewels with more than 200 carats of diamonds. Similarly, she completed her look by wearing a glittering choker and an emerald ring.

Sal kept her smoky eye and nude lip combo subtle, but striking and make her hair in a trendy, slicked-back bun.

Her team, which included stylist Kate Young, makeup artist Hung Vanngo, hairstylist Marissa Marino, and nail artist Tom Bachik, worked together to make her look stunning.
