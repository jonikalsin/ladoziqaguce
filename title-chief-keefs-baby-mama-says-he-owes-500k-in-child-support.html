Chief Keefs Baby Mama Says He Owes $500K In Child Support

Erica Early, the mother of one of Chief Keef’s nine children, has accused him of owing $500,000 in child support.

Early claims Keef has violated a court order after failing to pay $10,713 a month in child support, according to documents obtained by The Blast. The Glo Gang founder has allegedly racked up a debt of $471,910, plus interest.

The 43-year-old mother has requested a judge order Keef in court to explain his lack of payments. A hearing has been scheduled for later this month.

Early and Keef’s daughter Kimora Cozart was born in 2013. A judge originally ordered the drill rap pioneer to pay $2,500 a month in child support, but Early was later able to get the amount raised to $10,713.

Keef has continually dealt with issues regarding child support payments over the years. Last December, Early asked a judge to lock him up in jail over his failure to pay.

That same month, Keef admitted to fathering four previously unknown children with four different women. He was ordered to pay $564 per month to each mother.
