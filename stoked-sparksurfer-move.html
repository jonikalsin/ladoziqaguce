From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Stoked Sparksurfer (Japanese: ライトニングサーフライド Lightning Surf Ride) is a damage-dealing Electric-type Z-Move introduced in Generation VII. It is the exclusive Z-Move of Alolan Raichu and an upgraded version of its Thunderbolt.

Effect

Generation VII

Stoked Sparksurfer inflicts damage and paralyzes the target.

Generation VIII onwards

Stoked Sparksurfer cannot be selected in a battle.

Learnset

Alolan Raichu, or a Pokémon transformed into Alolan Raichu, can use Stoked Sparksurfer if it knows Thunderbolt, holds an Aloraichium Z, and if its Trainer wears a Z-RingSM or Z-Power RingUSUM.
