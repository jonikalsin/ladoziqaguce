Oliver Peck (Tattoo Artist) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Oliver Peck is an American tattoo artist, restaurateur, and reality TV personality. Along with singer Dave Navarro and tattoo artist Chris Nunez, Peck served as a judge for the Ink Master reality show for Seasons 1-13. He left the series after a backlash when previous images appeared of Peck in a black face. Known for his American style tattoos, he is part owner of Elm Street Tattoo in Dallas, Texas and owner of True Tattoo in Hollywood, California. His Tiki Loco restaurant is located in Deep Ellum, Dallas.
