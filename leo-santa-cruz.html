Biography

Leo Santa Cruz was born in Huetamo, Mexico on August 10, 1988. Bantamweight and featherweight boxer who has held the WBA super featherweight, WBC super bantamweight, and IBF bantamweight titles. In February 2017, he was ranked as the world’s 7th best pound-for-pound fighter in the world by BoxRec. He won the WBA super featherweight title in August 2015 after defeating Abner Mares. His amateur boxing career concluded in 2010 with a record of 148 wins and 7 losses.
