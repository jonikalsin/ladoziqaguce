Kortnei Johnson Road To Tokyo

Kortnei Johnson is was one of the top athletes running the 200M. In her illustrious college career she has the following accolades.

Collegiate Record Holder – 4x100m Relay – 42.05
2x NCAA Champion (2016 and 2018 4×1)
9x All-American
8x All-SEC
5x SEC Champion (2019 60m Dash, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019 4×1)
2019 SEC Spring Academic Honor Roll

In Kortnei's career she has overcame many obstacles and will continue to show her determination on the track!
