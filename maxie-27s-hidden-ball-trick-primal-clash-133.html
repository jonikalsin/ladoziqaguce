Maxie's Hidden Ball Trick (Japanese: マツブサの隠し玉 Matsubusa's Secret Weapon) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Primal Clash expansion.

Card text

You can play this card only when it is the last card in your hand.

Put a Pokémon from your discard pile onto your Bench. Then, draw 5 cards.

You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was released as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the English Primal Clash expansion, first originating from the Japanese Gaia Volcano expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Ken Sugimori, while the Full Art print features artwork by Hitoshi Ariga.

Gallery

Trivia

A ruling effective February 15, 2019 banned this card from the Expanded format due to a combo with Omastar or Kabutops, both from the Team Up expansion, causing a potentially devastating effect on the first turn.

Origin

Maxie is the leader of Team Magma in the Pokémon games.
