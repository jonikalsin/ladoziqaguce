Biography

Theo Von is best known as a comedian. Standup comedian and TV host who rose to fame in season four of NBC's Last Comic Standing, earning the title of Last Comic Downloaded. He was featured on Comedy Central's Reality Bites Back. He is also known as the founder of the word “crank texting,” which was used in CollegeHumor. He was born on March 19, 1980 in Covington, LA. He appeared alongside comedian Amy Schumer in Comedy Central's Reality Bites Back. He became recognized for the first time after competing on MTV's famous series Road Rules: Maximum Velocity Tour. He hosts a weekly podcast with Matthew Cole Weiss, Reportedly called Theo Von & Matthew Cole Weiss.
