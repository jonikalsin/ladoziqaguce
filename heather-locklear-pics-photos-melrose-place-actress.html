Heather Locklear is best known as an actress and you can see her photos here.

Heather Locklear, born September 25, 1961, is an American actress. Heather rose to fame on the dramatic, soap opera series ‘Melrose Place’ from 1993-1999, after she starred on the series ‘Dynasty’ from 1981-1989. Heather earned a slew of awards nominations for her work on ‘Melrose Place’ including four consecutive Golden Globe nominations for ‘Melrose Place.’ 

Heather’s personal life also made headlines. She was married to Tommy Lee from 1986-1993, then Bon Jovi guitarist Richie Sambora from 1994-2007. She had one daughter with Richie, named Ava, in 1997. After her divorce from Richie, she dated and became engaged to her ‘Melrose Place’ fellow alum Jack Wagner, but the two called off their engagement in November 2011.
