Natasha Yi’s Career and Net Worth

Natasha Yi is an American actress, model, and stunt woman who has a net worth of $1 million. She was born in Boston, Massachusetts in March 1979.

Yi started her career as a model for the television series The Price Is Right in 2005 and for the TV series The Price is Right Million Dollar Spectacular from 2005 to 2006. She later appeared in several films such as Rush Hour 2, Cradle 2 the Grave, The Onion Movie, Hell Ride, Thunder Over Reno, Street Playerz, and Speed-Dating.

Aside from acting, Yi has also appeared in various short films and videos such as Playboy: Girls of Mardi Gras, Playboy: American Beauties, Playboy’s Girlfriends and Mystique Presents H2Ohh. She also performed stunts for Kelly Hu in the 2002 movie The Scorpion King.

Yi has made guest appearances on TV shows like V.I.P., The Starter Wife, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, Picking Miss Octane, and Rules of Engagement.
