Tom Brady &amp; More Hollywood Life

good light, a brand that champions beauty beyond the binary, partnered with Opening Ceremony founder, Humberto Leon, to celebrate and acknowledge the successes of the AAPI community this weekend.

Notable guests included: Christine Ko (FXX’s Dave, Hulu’s Only Murders in the Building), Alan Yang (co-creator of Master of None, Loot, Forever), Poppy Liu (The Afterparty), Raymond Lee (NBC’s Quantum Leap), Ally Maki (Apple TV+ The Big Door Prize/Asian American Girl Club), Miki Ishikawa (The Falcon), Manny Jacinto (I Want You Back, The Good Place), Kara Wang (Top Gun: Maverick), Karan Soni (Deadpool 1,2,3), Kevin Woo (K-Pop star), Travis Atreo (Singer/Songwriter), Tyson Wong (actor/model), Michelle Phan (YouTube pioneer, founder of EM Cosmetics), Carol Lim (Creative Director, Opening Ceremony), Sarah Cheung (YouTube star), Carlo Panuncialman (Producer), Henry Lo (Councilman, Former Mayor of Monterey Park). 

The private dinner was held at Monarch (Leon’s restaurant), co-hosted by good light founder David Yi in support of the Asian Mental Health Collective and powered by D’USSE.
