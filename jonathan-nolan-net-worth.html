Jonathan Nolan Net Worth


At $20 million, Jonathan Nolan’s net worth is reported as substantial. He is acclaimed as a British-American screenwriter, television producer, director, and author, best known for creating the crime drama series Person of Interest as well as co-writing various screenplays with his brother, filmmaker Christopher Nolan, including The Dark Knight.

His significant works and achievements include Interstellar, The Dark Knight Rises, The Dark Knight, The Prestige, and Memento. Jonathan Nolan is also famed for his TV series, Person of Interest, and Westworld.

Jonathan Nolan was born in London, United Kingdom, on June 6, 1976, and holds a degree from Georgetown University. He is married to Lisa Joy and is the son of Christina and Brendan Nolan, with siblings Christopher Nolan and Matthew Francis Nolan.

Notably, he has won a Saturn Award and a Bram Stoker Award for Best Screenplay and has received numerous nominations, including for Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay and Hugo Award for Best Dramatic Presentation, Long Form, among others.

Jonathan Nolan is also recognized for his accomplished work as a writer, producer, director, actor, and in various other roles across the film and TV industry.
