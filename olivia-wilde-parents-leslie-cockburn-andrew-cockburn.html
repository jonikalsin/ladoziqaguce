Olivia Wilde parents: Leslie Cockburn, Andrew Cockburn

Olivia Jane Cockburn, better known by her stage name Olivia Wilde, is a New York-based actress, model, producer, director, and activist.

Her most well-known role is that of Dr. Remy in the medical drama television series House.’

Wilde is descended from a long history of gifted artists.

Her parents are also well-known in the arts.

Wilde grew up in Washington, DC, but spent her summers in Ireland as one of three siblings. Christopher Hitchens, a well-known writer, would occasionally babysit them.

Olivia Wilde parents: Meet Leslie Cockburn, Andrew Cockburn

Leslie Cockburn, Wilde’s mother, is an American journalist and producer for 60 Minutes. Andrew Cockburn, her father, is a journalist as well.

He is the son of British novelist and journalist Claud Cockburn. He was raised in Ireland after being born in London.

Olivia Savage, Cockburn’s daughter, has a net worth of $13 million, making her a wealthy heiress.
