Eric Montross Obituary, Death – The UNC basketball community is grieving the loss of a true Tar Heel legend, Eric Montross, who passed away at the age of 52. Montross, diagnosed with cancer in March 2023, had become not only an icon on the court but a cherished figure in Chapel Hill, where he continued to contribute to the Tar Heel legacy. Eric Montross played a pivotal role in UNC basketball history, representing the Tar Heels from 1990 to 1994 under the guidance of legendary coach Dean Smith.

During his time at Chapel Hill, Montross showcased his prowess as a formidable center and played an integral role in the team’s success. One of the crowning achievements of Montross’s illustrious career was his leadership as the starting center on the 1993 team that clinched the NCAA Championship. That season, he not only contributed to the team’s victory but earned several individual accolades, cementing his status as one of the standout players in UNC basketball history. During the championship run, Montross received first-team All-ACC honors and garnered recognition with NCAA East Region and Final Four all-tournament distinctions.

His senior year was marked by his inclusion in the prestigious John Wooden All-America team, a fitting tribute to a player who left an indelible mark on the college basketball landscape. Beyond his playing career, Montross continued to be an enduring presence in the UNC community. For 18 seasons, he lent his voice and expertise to the Tar Heel Sports Network, sharing his insights with fans and contributing to the vibrant basketball culture at UNC. The passing of Eric Montross leaves a void in the hearts of the UNC basketball family, but his legacy is etched in the annals of Tar Heel history. His contributions as a player, a mentor, and a broadcaster have left an enduring impact on the program.

The outpouring of grief from fans, players, and fellow broadcasters underscores the profound influence Montross had on the Tar Heel community. As the University of North Carolina mourns the loss of a beloved Tar Heel, the memories of Eric Montross’s contributions to the basketball program will continue to inspire future generations of players and fans alike. May he rest in peace, forever remembered as a true legend of UNC basketball.
