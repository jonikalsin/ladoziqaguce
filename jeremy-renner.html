Photos of the Marvel Actor Hollywood Life

Jeremy Lee Renner is one of the highest-grossing box-office stars of all time, known for his roles in the Marvel Universe and more. After making his film debut in 1995’s ‘National Lampoon’s Senior Trip,’ he would go on to find early success in the 2002 film, ‘Dahmer.’Jeremy Renner’s big break would come in 2009 in Katheryn Bigelow’s The Hurt Locker. Jeremy would earn critical acclaim, as well as several awards (including his first Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.) His role in The Town continued to garnish him critical praise. Yet, his biggest role, one that would define this stage of his career, would come in 2011. An uncredited cameo in the first Thor movie introduced the world to the character of Hawkeye. In 2011, Jeremy would join another beloved franchise as a part of Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol. He would return for the 2015 sequel, Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation. Jeremy was involved in a serious accident with a snow plow on New Years’ Day 2023. He was airlifted to a New Jersey hospital in critical condition.
